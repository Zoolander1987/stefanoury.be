Personal
Manifesto

01.

— Walk the Talk  —

Don't shout to be the loudest but choose actions above words — anytime. Don't make empty promises, put in the work and deliver. Always.

02.

— Be Humble  —

Sit down and be humble. Not in a Kendrick way but by taking pride in your work & by being self-aware. Only impress yourself, never stop learning and accept compliments without letting it go to your head.

03.

— Be a Challenger  —

Question everything and be open-minded. Take your professional obligation serious and never put other people in the position where you'll prescribe a solution without first fully diagnosing the challenge.
