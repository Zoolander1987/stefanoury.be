About DBV Architecten

DBV Architecten is a multidisciplinary company specialized in project construction, from business, office, and apartment buildings to industry, schools, care, and sports centers. DBV Architecten has become a safe bet among medium-sized architectural firms in Limburg.

Home Intro

The Brief

One of the main problems was that their old website did not convert and gave not enough sufficient to showcase their projects. The client trusted RCA with the rollout of DBV's new strategic future plan. This included a completely new brand strategy, a brand identity overhaul, and a new website. I helped with the redesign of the website that helps the company attract fresh extraordinary projects and give projects a new place to shine.

 

Framing the problem

First things first, we organized multiple discovery workshops to define the content and website structure. One of the biggest challenge was how to structure existing and new services. We quickly came to the realization that it was of great importance to create a website with a global feeling and a prioritization to showcase projects, services, and clear call-to-actions.

Design Progress

 

Early design explorations

The Solution

We redefined their website to a experience based portal. By doing so it could highlight the brand as a whole and strengthen its identity. We choose for a modern contemporary design with a strong focus on content with a lot of negative space to let all the element breath.
